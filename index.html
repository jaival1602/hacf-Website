About HACF

The Hindu Antyesti Care Foundation is created to help Hindu families cope with the rising cost of funerals in Ontario, Canada, with care, compassion, and dignity.

Community Support

Helping families during one of life’s most difficult moments through a shared community fund pool.

Hindu Last Rites

Designed for members who believe in funeral services through electric, natural gas, or propane crematoriums.

Ontario Based

Membership is open only to Hindus residing in Ontario, Canada.

Membership Structure

Please Note: This scheme will be launched only after registration of the first 100 members.

Membership is strictly individual. Every family member, from newborn to any age, must register separately.

Parents or guardians are responsible for registering minor children and paying their fees and collective contribution amounts.

One-Time Lifetime Registration

$200

Invest once, benefits for life.

  • Lifetime membership coverage
  • Simple one-time registration
  • Small ongoing community contribution when required
  • Protection from unexpected funeral financial stress

Funeral Benefit

HACF Will Take Care Of It

$5,500 Benefit

A fixed amount of $5,500 will be paid directly to the funeral home in Ontario only, through bank draft, cheque, or e-Transfer.

60-Day Waiting Period

Funeral benefit coverage begins after completion of a 60-day waiting period from the date of joining.

Outside Ontario Funeral

If funeral is held outside Ontario, the spouse/nominee receives a $200 registration refund plus $100, totaling $300.

Good Standing Rules

  • Members must keep all payments up to date.
  • A 15-day grace period is provided after payment notification.
  • Claims require completed waiting period and good standing status.

Claim Requirements

  • Death Certificate
  • Member ID or proof of membership
  • Claim must be reported within 24 hours of death
  • Fraud or misrepresentation may result in denial

Fast Claim Process

Designed to support funeral arrangements quickly and respectfully.

Report Within 24 Hours

The claim should be reported within 24 hours of death.

Approval in 24–48 Hours

The approval process will take approximately 24 to 48 hours.

Payment Within 7 Days

Payment will be made directly to the funeral home within 7 days.

Transparency & Reserve Fund

Quarterly Updates

Reports will be shared with members through WhatsApp and/or social media.

Annual Reports

Annual financial reports will be provided to maintain transparency.

Reserve Fund

Claims are paid first from the Reserve Fund. Use of funds requires 2/3 majority approval by the Board of Directors.
